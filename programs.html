Our Academic Programs

Discover a comprehensive educational journey designed to inspire curiosity, foster critical thinking, and prepare students for a lifetime of success.

Elementary School

Grades K-5

Building strong foundations in literacy, numeracy, and social skills through engaging, hands-on learning experiences that spark curiosity and a love for learning.

  • Literacy and Language Arts
  • Mathematics and Problem-Solving
  • Science Exploration
  • Social Studies and Citizenship
  • Arts, Music, and Physical Education
  • Social-Emotional Learning

Program Highlights

Small class sizes with 15:1 student-teacher ratio • Project-based learning • Weekly library and computer lab time • After-school enrichment clubs

Middle School

Grades 6-8

Developing critical thinking and academic skills while exploring diverse subjects and extracurricular interests in a supportive environment.

  • Advanced Mathematics (Pre-Algebra, Algebra)
  • Laboratory Sciences (Life, Earth, Physical)
  • Language Arts and Literature
  • World Languages (Spanish, French, Mandarin)
  • Technology and Computer Science
  • History and Civic Engagement

Program Highlights

STEM lab with 3D printers • Robotics club • Debate team • Annual science fair • Field trips and outdoor education

High School

Grades 9-12

College-preparatory curriculum with advanced placement courses, diverse electives, and comprehensive college counseling to ensure post-secondary success.

  • AP and Honors Courses (12+ offerings)
  • STEM Specialization Track
  • Humanities and Social Sciences
  • Fine Arts and Performing Arts
  • College Preparation and SAT/ACT Prep
  • Career Pathways and Internships

Program Highlights

98% college acceptance rate • Dual enrollment opportunities • College counseling from grade 9 • Scholarship guidance • Alumni mentoring network
