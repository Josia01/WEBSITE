About Our School

Founded in 1988, Twing Memorial Schools has been a cornerstone of educational excellence in our community. We honor the legacy of Dr. Eleanor Twing, a pioneering educator who believed in holistic, student-centered learning.

Our mission is to cultivate curious, compassionate, and capable learners who are prepared to thrive in an increasingly complex world. We balance academic rigor with character development, creativity, and critical thinking.
