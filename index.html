About Our School

Founded in 1988, Twing Memorial Schools has been a cornerstone of educational excellence. We honor the legacy of Dr. Eleanor Twing, a pioneering educator who believed in holistic, student-centered learning.

Our mission is to cultivate curious, compassionate, and capable learners who are prepared to thrive in an increasingly complex world.
